Medication Reminders

Personalized medication reminders with dosage instructions and timing alerts. Improve treatment adherence by ensuring patients never miss their medications.Ideal for chronic disease management, post-operative care, and pharmaceutical companies looking to enhance patient compliance rates and medication adherence programs.

Digital Medical Documents

Instant delivery of digital bills, prescriptions, and medical reports via WhatsApp. Provide patients with secure, accessible medical documents they can easily share and store.Essential for modern healthcare practices, hospitals, and diagnostic centers looking to digitize their patient communication and reduce paper-based workflows.
